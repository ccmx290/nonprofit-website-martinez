# Atelier Colima Nonprofit Website

## Mission Statement
Atelier Colima is a nonprofit organization and artist collective committed to the promotion and education of art and culture in the state of Colima through community-led classes and events. 

## Website Purpose
The purpose of this website is to inform visitors about our artistic and cultural events and classes, collaborate with and provide support to Colima artists, accept monetary and art supply donations, and serve as a digital presence to spread the word about our mission and garner support. 

## Target Audience
- Art enthusiasts
- Colima-based artists interested in collaborating/volunteering
- Members of the community seeking to engage in artistic events or cultural learning opportunities

## Planned Features
- Home page with a mission and impact overview, a contact form, and social media links
- About Us page with our history and information about our work
- Donation page with a payment form/link, and information on other ways to support our mission
- Events page
- Mobile-friendly and responsive design
